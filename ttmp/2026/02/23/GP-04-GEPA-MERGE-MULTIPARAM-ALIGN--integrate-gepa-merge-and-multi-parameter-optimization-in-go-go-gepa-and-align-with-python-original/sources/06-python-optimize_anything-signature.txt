  1011	    artifact, your evaluator, and any domain knowledge — and
  1012	    ``optimize_anything`` handles the **how**: prompt construction, reflection,
  1013	    candidate selection, and Pareto-efficient search.
  1014	
  1015	    **Three optimization modes** (determined by ``dataset`` / ``valset``):
  1016	
  1017	    1. **Single-Task Search** (``dataset=None, valset=None``):

  1020	       *E.g. circle packing, blackbox mathematical optimization.*
  1021

  1023	       Solve a batch of related problems with cross-task transfer.
  1024	       Insights from solving one help solve the others.
  1025	       ``valset`` defaults to ``dataset``.
  1026	       *E.g. CUDA kernel generation, multi-aspect SVG optimization.*
  1027	
  1028	    3. **Generalization** (``dataset=<list>, valset=<list>``):
  1029	       Build a skill that transfers to unseen problems.
